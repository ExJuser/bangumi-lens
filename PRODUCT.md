# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Anime viewers and Bangumi users who want to review public episode discussions, compare episodes within a subject, and generate local Chinese reports from Bangumi episode pages.

## Product Purpose

Bangumi Lens helps users search Bangumi animation subjects, choose episodes, analyze public episode comments, and keep local report history. Success means users can find the right subject and episode quickly, trust cached data behavior, and generate or revisit reports without ambiguity.

## Brand Personality

Focused, literary, restrained. The interface should feel like a task-focused reading and analysis tool rather than a marketing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative SaaS dashboards, oversized landing-page composition, playful novelty controls, and unclear cache or destructive actions.

## Design Principles

- Keep search, selection, and report generation controls explicit and predictable.
- Treat Bangumi IDs, episode IDs, and cache keys as user-trust boundaries.
- Prefer dense, scannable panels over decorative layouts.
- Make risky or state-changing actions visibly distinct without interrupting routine workflows.
- Preserve existing local history and user-generated report state.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic controls, visible focus states, clear button labels, sufficient contrast, and motion only when it communicates state. Respect reduced-motion preferences where animation is present.
